Additional Resources
A. SHOULDER HEIGHT IQ — If the ball contacts above shoulder at pace, it's almost always out. Contact point is your leading cue.
B. THE WIND-UP CUE — A big paddle wind-up = pace. Big pace + high contact = out. Read the wind-up before the ball crosses the net.
C. READ THE SOURCE — Watch where the ball is being struck: deep in the court + hard swing = likely out. Kitchen shot + soft touch = stay ready to volley or let it bounce.
